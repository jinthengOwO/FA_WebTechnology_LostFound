About Our Platform

A digital solution for a connected campus community.

Our Mission

The Campus Lost & Found System is dedicated to providing a streamlined, secure, and user-friendly digital platform. We aim to help students effortlessly recover misplaced belongings and empower helpful individuals to return found items efficiently.

Key Features

  • Seamless Browsing: Advanced search and filtering tools to locate items quickly.
  • Easy Reporting: A straightforward form to post missing or found items in seconds.
  • Status Management: Real-time tracking with "Active" and "Resolved" status updates.
